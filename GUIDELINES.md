# Flux Agency Video Generator — User Guide

## What is this tool?

**Flux Agency Video Generator** automates the creation of cinematic video assets and prompts from a script. It follows a professional workflow:

1. **Script Processing**: AI analyzes your script and breaks it into logical scenes.
2. **Character Lock**: Reference photos ensure consistent faces across all scenes.
3. **Asset Generation**: High-quality images and video prompts are generated for each scene.

---

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Step 1 — Upload Reference Photos (Optional)
Upload 1-4 photos to "lock" the face of your subject (e.g., CEO, actor). This ensures the character looks the same in every generated scene.

### Step 2 — Paste Your Script
Paste your dialogue or script into the **"Video Script"** box. The AI will automatically segment it into 4–8 scenes.

### Step 3 — Configure Settings
- **Tone/Style**: Choose the emotional direction (Professional, Luxury, etc.).
- **Aspect Ratio**: 9:16 (TikTok/Shorts), 16:9 (YouTube), or 1:1.
- **Image Model**: Nano Banana Pro is recommended for the highest quality.
- **Video Model**: Veo 3.0 provides cinematic prompt output.

### Step 4 — Generate Prompts
Click **"Generate Options / Scene"**. AI will generate 2 distinct visual directions for every scene, including image prompts and camera movement descriptions for video.

### Step 5 — Generate Images
Click **"Gen Image (Nano Banana)"** on your preferred scene option. Use the **Copy** button to manually adjust prompts if needed.

### Step 6 — Export
- **Export Selected**: Copy only the prompts you've chosen.
- **Export All**: Copy the entire project breakdown.
- **Download**: Save images (PNG) directly to your device.

---

## Tips for Success

- **Use Reference Photos**: Face-locking significantly improves brand consistency.
- **English Prompts**: The models perform best with English instructions (set as default).
- **Iterate**: If a scene isn't perfect, click generate again or manually tweak the prompt using the copy button.

## FAQ

**Why are images failing?**
The API might be busy. Wait 10 seconds and try again.

**How do I save my work?**
Everything is cleared on refresh. Make sure to download your assets or copy your prompts before closing the tab.
